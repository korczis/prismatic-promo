Executive Summary

Prismatic Platform's Executive Security Dashboard transforms cybersecurity from technical complexity into strategic business intelligence. Real-time risk metrics, financial impact analysis, and board-ready reporting enable C-suite executives to make informed decisions, communicate with stakeholders, and demonstrate cybersecurity ROI.

Dashboard Capabilities:

  • Real-time Risk Visibility: Strategic cybersecurity metrics updated continuously
  • Financial Impact Tracking: Quantified business value and cost avoidance metrics
  • Compliance Scorecards: Automated regulatory compliance monitoring and reporting
  • Stakeholder Communication: Board-ready presentations and investor materials

Executive Value: Transform cybersecurity from cost center to strategic advantage with measurable business outcomes and risk-based decision making.
