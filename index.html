What problem are you solving?

Each gateway leads to platform capabilities organized by the operational challenge you face -- not by feature taxonomy.

Accountability

Decisions lack traceable evidence chains. Confidence is asserted, not derived.

Drift

Systems degrade below the threshold of observation. Changes compound silently.

Evidence

Intelligence from multiple sources lacks epistemic rigor. Trust is assumed, not measured.

Attack Surface

External perimeter keeps changing. Shadow IT, acquisitions, and misconfigurations go undetected.

Compliance

Regulations evolve faster than audit cycles. Point-in-time assessments decay immediately.

Scale

Manual processes that worked for 10 entities collapse at 100. Depth is sacrificed for throughput.

Trust

Counterparties present polished surfaces. You need to verify what is underneath.

Visibility

Component monitoring exists. System-level understanding does not. Cross-domain failures go undetected.

What Prismatic is not

Not a hosted SaaS product. Prismatic is infrastructure that runs on your machines. Deployment requires Elixir runtime and PostgreSQL.

Not a general-purpose AI assistant. LLMs are used for specific agent reasoning tasks within the platform. Prismatic is not a chatbot.

Not a replacement for human analysts. It produces structured, provenance-tracked evidence for humans to make better-informed decisions. It does not make decisions autonomously.

Not a security product in isolation. EASM and security ratings are capabilities within a broader intelligence infrastructure. They gain their value from integration with the full platform.
