Czech Registries

The Czech Republic maintains an unusually comprehensive network of public registries, making it one of the most transparent jurisdictions in the European Union for corporate due diligence. The Prismatic Platform provides deep integration with the following Czech government data sources:

SourceRegistryKey Data
ARESAdministrative Register of Economic SubjectsCompany search, ICO/DIC lookup, business activities
Justice.czCommercial Register (Obchodni rejstrik)Company filings, statutory bodies, ownership structure
RZPTrade Licensing Register (Zivnostensky rejstrik)Trade licenses, business permits, authorized activities
ISIRInsolvency Register (Insolvencni rejstrik)Insolvency proceedings, creditor claims, resolution status
